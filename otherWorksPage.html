Other Works

Weapons

I modeled this kukri knife in Maya, and I textured it in Substance Painter. I spent a lot of time using stencils to add dirt and oxidization texture to the blade. I wanted it to look like it had been burried in wet ground for a long time.

The firearms were modeled and textured in Blender. I wanted to focus on keeping them low-poly, and giving them realistic materials to keep them grounded.

Solitaire Siege User Interface

I designed this user interface for a tower defense game called Solitaire Siege, which I developed with a team of students at Boise State University. I created this asset in Photoshop. My team wanted a color scheme to match the Bicicle playing cards' theme. We also decided that we wanted to go for a steampunk-inspired aesthetic, too. To combine them, I went for some smoother, organic shapes, while adding in a few exposed gears.
