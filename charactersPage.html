Character Development

Charlie Holloway, PI

Charlie is the playable character in my independently developed game, The Casebook of Charlie Holloway. I was inspired by the private detectives of 1950s film noir, but I wanted to give him a unique outfit that would fit in the world of Baz Luhrmann's The Great Gatsby.

Acolyte

The Acolyte was the first finished human body I modeled and textured. It's a character I designed for a cancelled game, which involved fighting street gangs inspired by different musical sub-genres. In the case of The Acolyte, the inspiration was dark techno. I gave him mechanical eyes and headphones fused into his skull and a green on black gridded tank-top to lean into the techno feel. For the darker vibe, I gave him occult-inspired tattoos, black lipstick and nails, and green tips in his dreads.

Astro Rifleman

This was the first humanoid character I ever finished. I did the modeling, texturing, and rigging in Blender. I wanted to create an infantry troop that could exist in a sci-fi world, but I wanted to keep it grounded and practical. To achieve this, I gave him a traditional astronaut look, but included an integrated plate carrier and soft armor pads. I also gave him an AR-style rifle.
