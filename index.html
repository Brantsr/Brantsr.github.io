Welcome!

Hello! I'm Brants, an aspiring 3D artist focused on environment and character design for games. I'm passionate about creating stylized, yet grounded environments that support storytelling and player exploration. My goal is to contribute to projects that prioritize atmosphere, depth, and meaningful player experiences. I have experience working with a variety of software, including Blender, Maya, Photoshop, Substance Painter, Unreal Engine, and Unity, and I'm always eager to learn new tools and techniques to improve my craft.

I'm currently a student in the Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technologies (GIMM) program at Boise State University, where I am honing my skills in 3D modeling, texturing, and environment design. Previously, I have worked at movie theaters, and even did an enlistment in the US Marine Corps, but I am excited to pursue a career in game development and bring my creative visions to life.
