Inebu-Hedj

Inebu-Hedj was the name for ancient Memphis in Egypt. I created this environment in Unreal Engine 5, focusing on different 'hero assets' I created in-engine. These include the city wabet, the palace, and the Temple of Ptah. I also wanted to create a sense of scale through the addition of commoners' houses and foliage. I also experimented with a terrain material that changes based on angle- sand for flatter areas, and stone for steeper ones. I worked with the camera tools in-engine to create a short video walkthrough of the environment.

The Underpass

The Underpass was my first attempt at creating a 3D environment. I modeled it in Blender as a concept art piece for a canceled game project. The textures were created using Blender's shader nodes, and I drew the graffiti in Gimp with a mouse. I wanted to create an urban environment that felt lived-in, yet bright and hopeful.
